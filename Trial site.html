                          About us                          

                         Welcome to The Ghost Tour

Our goal is to bring pool to everyone, anywhere, anytime!!

You will be playing 5 racks of 10 ball ghost with a max

score of 50 points. This will be done via your phone,

tablet or laptop. You will live stream your match via 

this site. Players will have a handicap in most of these

tournaments, so everyone has a fair chance of

winning. Before you can play you will have to create

a player profile. The profile is very simple and easy.

There will be no limit on the number of players that
 
enter the tournaments and yes a player may enter
 
multiple times, but only the highest score will be counted.
