How To
Library
   Welcome to The Ghost Tour!!!  The online pool tournament that is held worldwide. Anyone can play from anywhere, anytime. There are 4 different tournaments held monthly you will stream yourself using a smart phone, tablet or laptop and internet connection. Each tournament will be 10 ball ghost with some different rules. You will play 5 racks and will have a score after its over. These scores will determine your position in the tournament. Don't worry if you are not a pro, because our handicap system helps equalize it to where everyone has a chance too win. There will be a separate handicapped tournaments for 7', 8' & 9' tables plus an open tournament. The open tournament is a non handicapped event where you can pick any table size you choose. Best score wins. We have a very detailed way to decide tie breakers. There is a review board set in place to watch for anything that may turn up and their ruling will be the final decision. The fee is $20 per entry and yes you can enter multiple times. $10 dollars of each entry will go straight back to the payout. The other $10 will be split up between a fund for a year end tournament, promotions and fees. We want to make sure you know everything we do and we want everyone to know just how fair these tournaments are. Pool players for pool players!!!! Lets make this sport stronger than ever!!!!
